Cultivate Consulting
This is the landing page for Cultivate, a boutique marketing consultancy built on the belief that good marketing should feel as intentional as the businesses it represents.
After years of working across customer service, case management, research, and brand strategy — and completing a Master's in Information Design & Strategy from Northwestern University — I built Cultivate to offer small businesses and mission-driven brands the kind of strategic, personalized marketing support that's usually reserved for companies with big budgets.
Cultivate specializes in social media strategy, brand audits, content development, and digital presence consulting. Every engagement is rooted in research, driven by story, and designed to actually move the needle.
This page exists as a home base — a place for potential clients to learn what Cultivate does, understand the approach, and take the first step toward working together.
